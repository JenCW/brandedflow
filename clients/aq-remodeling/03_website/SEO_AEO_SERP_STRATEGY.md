## üìä What is SEO, AEO, and SERP?

### **SEO (Search Engine Optimization)**
- Making your website visible in Google search results
- Using keywords customers search for
- Optimizing content so Google understands what you offer

### **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)**
- Optimizing content to directly answer questions people ask
- Example: "Do I need a full remodel or just a plumber?" ‚Üí Your Trade Services page answers this
- Helps you appear in Google's "People Also Ask" sections

### **SERP (Search Engine Results Page)**
- The page Google shows when someone searches
- Your goal: Appear in the top 10 results (Page 1)
- Can include: Regular listings, local pack, featured snippets, rich results

## üöÄ AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Strategy

### **What is AEO?**
Optimizing content to directly answer questions people ask Google. When Google shows your content as the answer, you get:
- Featured snippets (answer box at top of results)
- "People Also Ask" appearances
- Voice search results (Alexa, Siri, Google Assistant)

### **Questions Your Site Now Answers:**

1. **"Do I need a full remodel or just a plumber?"**
   - **Answer Location:** Trade Services page
   - **Content:** "You don't need a full project - we offer individual trade work"
   - **AEO Impact:** Appears when people ask this question

2. **"What trade services do you offer?"**
   - **Answer Location:** Trade Services page
   - **Content:** Complete list of 17 trade services
   - **AEO Impact:** Comprehensive answer = featured snippet potential

3. **"Are you appointment only?"**
   - **Answer Location:** All pages
   - **Content:** "Available 7 days a week, by appointment only"
   - **AEO Impact:** Direct answer to appointment questions

4. **"What areas do you serve?"**
   - **Answer Location:** Contact page, footer
   - **Content:** "Serving all of Orange County"
   - **AEO Impact:** Local search optimization

5. **"Are you licensed and insured?"**
   - **Answer Location:** Statistics section, all pages
   - **Content:** "100% Licensed & Insured"
   - **AEO Impact:** Trust signal for search queries

### **How AEO Helps You:**
- **Featured Snippets:** Your content appears in the answer box (position 0)
- **Voice Search:** When people ask Alexa/Siri, your content can be read
- **Better Rankings:** Google favors content that answers questions
- **Higher Click-Through:** Featured snippets get 8.6% of all clicks
