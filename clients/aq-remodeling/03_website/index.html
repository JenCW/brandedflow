Need Just One Trade?

Not every project requires a complete remodel. Sometimes you just need a plumber, electrician, drywall contractor, or other specific trade work. AQ Remodeling offers individual trade services throughout Orange County, so you can get exactly what you need without committing to a full project.

Whether it's a plumbing repair, electrical upgrade, drywall installation, or any other trade service, we provide expert work as standalone jobs. Our licensed and insured tradespeople deliver the same quality craftsmanship whether it's a single trade job or part of a larger project.

Questions?

Find answers to common questions about our remodeling process

How long does a typical remodel take? +
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A bathroom remodel typically takes 2-3 weeks, while kitchen remodels take 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.
Do you handle permits and inspections? +
Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate inspections with local authorities to ensure your project meets all building codes and regulations.
What is your payment structure? +
We typically work with a deposit to begin work, progress payments at key milestones, and final payment upon project completion. We provide transparent, detailed estimates upfront.
