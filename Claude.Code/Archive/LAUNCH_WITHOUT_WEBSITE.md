# Launch Without a Website: Your Week 1 Action Plan

## The Truth About Websites

**You don't need a website to get your first clients. But you DO need one within 2 weeks.**

Most solopreneurs waste weeks "perfecting" their website before launch. They overthink every word, redesign 47 times, wait for professional photos, and never actually launch.

**Meanwhile, competitors with decent LinkedIn profiles and Calendly links are booking clients.**

Your first 3 clients come from:
1. People you know who trust you
2. LinkedIn connections who resonate with your story
3. Referrals from the above

**ALL of that happens before your website matters.**
