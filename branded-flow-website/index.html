DIY vs. Managed: What's the Difference?

DIY is great if:

  • You have time to manage it yourself
  • You enjoy tech and want to learn
  • You're on a tight budget

Managed is perfect if:

  • You want to focus on your work (not tech)
  • You want systems that get BETTER every month
  • You'd rather pay monthly than DIY

With Managed, we actively improve performance, add new automations, and optimize based on data—not just keep things running.
