### 6. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) for AI Search

AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE) prioritize:

**What to Do:**
- [ ] Add FAQ section to your site answering common questions
- [ ] Use clear, concise language (AI can parse it better)
- [ ] Structure content with clear headings
- [ ] Include "how to" and "what is" content
- [ ] Add table of contents to long pages
- [ ] Use bullet points and lists (AI loves structured data)

**FAQ Section to Add:**
```
- What is marketing automation?
- How much does AI receptionist cost?
- Do I need technical skills to use your systems?
- What's the difference between DIY and Managed?
- How long does setup take?
- Do you work with businesses outside Irvine?
```
