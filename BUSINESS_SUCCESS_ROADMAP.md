## EMERGENCY PLAN (When Things Get Tough)

### If You're Not Getting Leads:
1. Double down on LinkedIn (post 2x daily)
2. Reach out personally to 10 people per day
3. Offer free audit/consultation
4. Ask every client for 2 referrals
5. Partner with complementary businesses

### If You're Overwhelmed:
1. Pause new clients temporarily
2. Systemize what's causing pain
3. Raise prices (charge more, serve fewer)
4. Get help (VA, even 5 hours/week)
5. Remember why you're doing this

### If Imposter Syndrome Hits:
1. Review your testimonials
2. Look at your client results
3. Remember: You're helping people
4. Talk to another solopreneur
5. Journal your wins

### If Cash Flow is Tight:
1. Offer payment plans
2. Do quick-win services (logo, audit)
3. Reach out for project work
4. Review expenses (cut non-essentials)
5. Remember: This is temporary

**You've overcome COVID, raised 4 kids, and started at 50. You can handle whatever comes.**
